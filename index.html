Drink Efficiently. BeerSwift provides faster, easier beer check-ins for Untappd. Spend less time on the phone and more time drinking and socializing.

Features

Beer Queue

View a simple list of all the beers you've drank. Easily add/remove them. Check them in all at once!


Multiple Check-in Options

  • 1. Quick Add: Search for beer, rate it, specify number drank.
  • 2. Barcode Scanning: Scan the beer label. Coming Version 2.0.
  • 3. Snap Photo: Take a pic of the beer & it's name is automatically identified! Coming Version 2.0.

More Social

Spend less time on the phone. Quickly add a beer then get back to your conversation!

Offline Support

Queue up beers then check them in later, at your convenience.
